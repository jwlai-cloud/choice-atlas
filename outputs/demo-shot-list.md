## Non-negotiable story

Choice Atlas helps people facing an uncertain life dilemma understand the concerns and possibilities around each route. GPT-5.6 maps the provided dilemma live into validated categories; it does not predict, recommend, or conduct external research. Codex accelerated the product’s visual system, contract, integration, tests, and delivery.
