## Non-negotiable story

Choice Atlas helps people facing an uncertain life dilemma understand the concerns and possibilities around each route. GPT-5.6 maps the provided dilemma live into validated categories; it does not predict, recommend, or conduct external research. Codex accelerated the product’s visual system, contract, integration, tests, and delivery.

## Capture rules

- Do not record the judge-code entry screen. Begin recording only after the browser session is unlocked.
- Keep the browser at 100% zoom and capture at 1920×1080. Do not letterbox a mobile viewport inside a desktop canvas.
- Use the active UI state as the visual explanation. Narration should not introduce a concept before the cursor reveals it.
- If live mapping takes longer than three seconds, cut from the progress state directly to the completed Ground stage. The edit must not imply a pre-rendered result is live.
